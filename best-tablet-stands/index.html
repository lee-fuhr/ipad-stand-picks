A good tablet stand turns your iPad or Android slate into a hands-free workstation. Whether you’re following a recipe in the kitchen, taking video calls, or using your tablet as a second monitor, a stand keeps your screen at eye level and frees up desk space. The right choice depends on where you’ll use it most, a static desk setup calls for different priorities than a grab-and-go travel stand. When shopping, focus on three things: stability (wide bases and rubber grips prevent tipping), adjustability (multiple viewing angles let you find the perfect position), and build quality (metal frames last longer than plastic). Most adjustable stands fold flat, but weight and footprint vary. Below, we’ve picked the best models for every use and budget.
