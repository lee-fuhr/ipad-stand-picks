How we choose our picks

We do not run our own timed lab tests. Instead, we comb through thousands of verified buyer reviews across major retailers, read hands-on evaluations from reputable tech publications, and track which models hold up after six months to a year of daily use. The most common failure points, hinge looseness, rubber feet peeling off, and wobble when tapping the screen, are exactly what we flag. We also look for patterns in long-term owner feedback: a stand that collects dust? Dealbreaker. One that still feels tight after a year? That’s a keeper. This approach lets us recommend stands that perform well in the real world, not just out of the box. Every pick on this list has been vetted by hundreds of actual users, not a single influencer post.
